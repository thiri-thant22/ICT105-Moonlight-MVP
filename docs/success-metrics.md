# Success Metrics and Decision Rules

## 1. Success Metrics

| Metric ID | Metric | Target | How to Measure | Related Requirement |
|------------|-------------------------------|----------------|--------------------------------------|----------------|
| M-01 | Task completion rate | 80% or higher | Users complete all assigned tasks | FR-03, FR-05 |
| M-02 | Expense recording success | 90% or higher | Successfully saved expense records | FR-03 |
| M-03 | Dashboard understanding | 80% or higher | Users correctly understand spending summary | FR-05 |
| M-04 | Navigation success | 80% or higher | Users move between pages without assistance | FR-01 |
| M-05 | Overall usability rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating | MVP Prototype |

## 2. Decision Rules

| Result | Decision |
|--------|----------|
| All success metrics meet the target | Continue developing the prototype. |
| One or two metrics do not meet the target | Improve the interface, labels, or navigation and test again. |
| Most metrics do not meet the target | Review user requirements, redesign important screens, and repeat usability testing. |
