# Customer Discovery Summary

## Project Title
Budget Tracker

## Target Users Studied
- University Students
- Young Professionals

## Number of Responses / Interviews
10 Respondents
- 5 University Students
- 5 Young Professionals

## Main Repeated Problem
Most respondents reported that they do not regularly track their daily expenses and often spend more money than planned.

## Main Current Workarounds
- Recording expenses in notebooks
- Using mobile notes applications
- Using Excel or Google Sheets
- Relying on memory

## Strongest Evidence Found
Most respondents mentioned that they often forget where their money was spent and sometimes run out of budget before the end of the month.

## Weak Evidence or Unclear Findings
Some respondents already use spreadsheets or notes successfully, so more research is needed to understand which features are most important for different users.

## What We Must Revise
The team should focus on creating a simple and user-friendly expense tracking system. Advanced features should be considered only after validating the core problem.

## Decision for Next Lab
- Continue with same problem direction

## Updated Problem Statement
University students and young professionals often struggle to manage their personal finances because they do not consistently track their daily expenses. This leads to overspending, poor budget awareness, and difficulty saving money.
