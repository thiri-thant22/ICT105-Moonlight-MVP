# Business Model Canvas

## Project Title
Budget Tracker

## 1. Customer Segments
University students and young professionals who want to manage their daily expenses, monthly allowance, salary, and personal budget.

## 2. Value Proposition
Budget Tracker helps users record expenses quickly, monitor their spending, and avoid overspending. It provides a simple alternative to notebooks, phone notes, and spreadsheets by showing expense lists, spending summaries, and remaining budget in one place.

## 3. Channels
Users can access the product through a web-based application. The project can be promoted through university classmates, social media, student groups, and word-of-mouth recommendations.

## 4. Customer Relationships
The product maintains interaction with users through a simple and user-friendly interface, confirmation messages, dashboard summaries, and easy expense management features such as search, edit, and delete.

## 5. Revenue Streams / Sustainability Logic
For the MVP, the Budget Tracker will be free for users. Its value comes from helping students and young professionals manage money more effectively and reduce overspending. In the future, the product could use a freemium model with advanced reports, reminders, or premium budgeting features.

## 6. Key Resources
- Team members and assigned roles
- GitHub repository
- Wireframes and clickable prototype
- Web development tools
- Local Storage or database for expense data
- User feedback from interviews and usability testing

## 7. Key Activities
- Designing wireframes and user interface screens
- Developing the Budget Tracker prototype
- Creating Add Expense, Expense List, Dashboard, Search/Filter, Edit, and Delete features
- Testing the prototype with users
- Collecting feedback and improving the design

## 8. Key Partners
- University classmates and students who provide feedback
- Lecturers and tutors who provide guidance
- GitHub for version control and collaboration
- Figma or similar design tools for wireframes and prototypes
- Team members who contribute to development, design, documentation, and testing

## 9. Cost Structure
- Team time and effort
- Internet connection
- Web hosting, if needed
- Development tools and design tools
- Database or cloud storage costs, if used
- Maintenance and future feature improvements


## Final Note
The Business Model Canvas supports the Budget Tracker MVP by focusing on students and young professionals who need a simple way to manage expenses. The MVP features—Add Expense, Expense List, Dashboard Summary, Search/Filter, Edit, and Delete—directly solve the user problems identified during customer discovery. The prototype will validate whether users find the application easier and more useful than their current methods, such as notebooks, phone notes, or Excel.
