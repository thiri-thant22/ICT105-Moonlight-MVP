Project: Budget Tracker

The Budget Tracker system helps students and young professionals manage their daily expenses. Every planned wireframe and prototype feature is linked to the system requirements and user stories to ensure that the final product addresses real user needs identified during customer discovery. The prototype includes a homepage, expense submission form, expense records, detailed expense view, dashboard summary, and an admin management page, all of which are directly connected to the functional requirements (FR-01 to FR-12) and the project's MVP scope.
